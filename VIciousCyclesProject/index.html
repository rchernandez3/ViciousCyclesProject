Repairing Raleigh's love for bikes

Located in downtown Raleigh NC, we want to provide an easier way for bike owners in the area to get their bikes fixed. We also want to make it easier for people to have access to a bicycle without having to pay a premium by offering rentals and used bikes at a discounted price. We are here to bring bicycles to the heart of North Carolina.

HOW WE OPPERATE

  • BRING YOUR BIKE IN

    • We can check your bike for free and give you an estimate.

  • TUNE UPS & OTHER SERVICES

    • The wait for any major services will take about 3 weeks. Any small repairs such as a tube replacement can be done in the same day.

  • PARTS & ACCESSORIES

    • We offer many bike parts and accessories to personalize your bike the way you want.
